Cheese Pizza Recipe

Description

A classic cheese pizza with a crispy crust, tangy tomato sauce, and gooey melted cheese.

Ingredients

  • 1 pizza dough
  • 1 cup pizza sauce
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Optional toppings: pepperoni, bell peppers, onions, olives

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness.
  3. Transfer the rolled dough to a pizza stone or baking sheet.
  4. Spread the pizza sauce evenly over the dough.
  5. Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella cheese evenly over the sauce.
  6. Sprinkle grated Parmesan cheese on top of the mozzarella.
  7. Add any additional toppings if desired.
  8. Bake in the preheated oven for 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly.
  9. Remove from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before slicing.
  10. Enjoy your homemade cheese pizza!
