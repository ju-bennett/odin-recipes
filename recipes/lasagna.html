Lasagna Recipe

Description

A classic Italian dish made with layers of pasta, meat sauce, and cheese.

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 3 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 2 cups marinara sauce
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 egg
  • Salt and pepper to taste

    • Instructions

    1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
    2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
    3. In a skillet, cook the ground beef over medium heat until browned. Season with salt and pepper.
    4. In a bowl, mix the ricotta cheese, egg, and half of the Parmesan cheese. Season with salt and pepper.
    5. Spread a thin layer of marinara sauce on the bottom of a baking dish.
    6. Layer 4 lasagna noodles over the sauce. Spread one-third of the ricotta mixture over the noodles, followed by one-third of the mozzarella cheese and one-third of the meat sauce. Repeat this process two more times.
    7. Top with remaining noodles, marinara sauce, mozzarella cheese, and Parmesan cheese.
    8. Cover with aluminum foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove foil and bake for an additional 15 minutes or until cheese is bubbly and golden brown.
    9. Let it cool for 10 minutes before serving.
