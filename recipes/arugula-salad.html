Arugula Salad Recipe

Description

A fresh and vibrant arugula salad with a zesty lemon vinaigrette, perfect for a light meal or side dish.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups fresh arugula
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/4 cup red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup feta cheese, crumbled
  • 1/4 cup walnuts, toasted

    Instructions

    1. In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, lemon juice, salt, and pepper to make the vinaigrette.
    2. In a large bowl, combine the arugula, cherry tomatoes, red onion, feta cheese, and walnuts.
    3. Drizzle the vinaigrette over the salad and toss gently to combine.
    4. Serve immediately and enjoy!
